Cable Types & Standards

Cat6 Ethernet Cable

Speed: Up to 10 Gbps (short distances)
Max Length: 100 meters (328 feet)
Connector: RJ45
Use Case: Desktop to switch, patch panel connections

Fiber Optic Cable

Types: Single-mode (long distance), Multi-mode (short distance)
Speed: 10 Gbps to 100+ Gbps
Max Length: Up to 40km+ (single-mode)
Connector: LC, SC, ST
Use Case: Building-to-building, core switch connections

SFP/SFP+ Module

SFP: 1 Gbps transceiver
SFP+: 10 Gbps transceiver
Purpose: Convert electrical to optical signal
Use Case: Uplink ports on switches
